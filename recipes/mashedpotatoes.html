Mashed Potatoes

Description

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Broth Concentrate
  • Butter
  • Sour Cream
  • Cheese
  • Chives
  • Salt&Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop potatoes
  2. Add Potatoes to pot with enought water to cover by 1 inch.
  3. Add broth concentrate to potato/water pot
  4. bring to boil, boil approximately 15 minutes or until tender
  5. measure out toppings to taste
  6. pour out most of the potato water, mash potato with fork
  7. Add and mix in toppings
  8. enjoy!
